publications

Publications by Yuxiang Lin in reversed chronological order.

2025

  1. ArXiv
    Why We Feel: Breaking Boundaries in Emotional Reasoning with Multimodal Large Language Models
    Yuxiang Lin, Jingdong Sun, Zhi-Qi Cheng, and 7 more authors
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2504.07521, 2025
    Abs HTML PDF Code

    This paper presents EIBench, a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating Vision-Language Large Models’ ability to interpret and reason about emotions across multiple modalities.

2024

  1. NeurIPS
    Emotion-LLaMA: Multimodal Emotion Recognition and Reasoning with Instruction Tuning
    Zebang Cheng, Zhi-Qi Cheng, Jun-Yan He, and 5 more authors
    In Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2024
    Abs PDF Code

    This paper introduces Emotion-LLaMA, a multimodal large language model for emotion recognition and reasoning with instruction tuning. The model achieves state-of-the-art performance on multiple emotion recognition benchmarks.

  2. CVIU
    Invisible Gas Detection: An RGB-Thermal Cross Attention Network and A New Benchmark
    Jue Wang, Yuxiang Lin, Qi Zhao, and 4 more authors
    Computer Vision and Image Understanding, 2024
    Abs HTML PDF Code

    This paper proposes an RGB-Thermal Cross Attention Network (RT-CAN) for invisible gas detection and introduces a new benchmark dataset for this task.

2023

  1. ACMMM
    Semi-Supervised Multimodal Emotion Recognition with Expression MAE
    Zebang Cheng, Yuxiang Lin, Zhaoru Chen, and 6 more authors
    In Proceedings of the 31st ACM International Conference on Multimedia (ACM MM), 2023
    Abs PDF Slides

    This paper presents Expression MAE, a semi-supervised approach for multimodal emotion recognition that achieved runner-up in the MER 2023 Grand Challenge at ACM MM.
